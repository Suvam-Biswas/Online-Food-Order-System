About Us

Who We Are

Yummy_Site> is a family-owned and operated food website that was founded in 2005 by Suvam and Abhinaba. Growing up in an Indian household, they developed a passion for cooking and a love for sharing their creations with others.

What We Do

At Yummy_Site>, we specialize in traditional Indian cuisine. Our menu features a variety of dishes(Veg and Non-Veg), fastfoods and desserts that are made from scratch using only the freshest ingredients available. We also offer catering services for events and special occasions.

Our Philosophy

At Yummy_Site>, we believe that food is not just something you eat, but something that brings people together. We strive to create a warm and inviting atmosphere where our customers can relax, enjoy a delicious meal, and make memories with their loved ones.

Our Commitment to Quality

What sets Yummy_Site> apart from other food websites is our commitment to quality. We take pride in using only the best ingredients and preparing each dish with care. Our chefs are highly skilled and experienced, and they are passionate about creating culinary masterpieces that will delight your taste buds.

Our Mission

Our mission at Yummy_Site> is to provide our customers with an exceptional dining experience that exceeds their expectations. We are dedicated to serving delicious food, providing excellent service, and creating a welcoming atmosphere that will keep our customers coming back for more.
